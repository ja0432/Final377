How to Use the App

1. Enter the name of the city in the input box.

2. Click the "Get Weather" button to fetch the current weather information.

3. View the weather details like temperature, humidity, wind speed, and weather status.

4. Based on the weather, receive outfit suggestions like whether to wear a jacket, T-shirt, etc.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • Why is my city not showing up? Make sure to type the city name correctly, including spaces and capitalization. Also, check if the city is recognized by the Weather.gov API.
  • How accurate is the outfit advice? The outfit recommendations are based on general weather conditions (e.g., cold temperatures suggest a jacket). The app does not provide personalized fashion advice but suggests clothing based on weather patterns.
