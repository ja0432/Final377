Project Overview

This app helps users choose the right clothes based on real-time weather conditions by providing dynamic outfit suggestions based on the weather forecast.

Technology

The app pulls weather data from the Weather.gov API to provide accurate and up-to-date weather conditions. This information is then processed to suggest appropriate clothing for users based on the current temperature and weather status (e.g., sunny, rainy, etc.).
